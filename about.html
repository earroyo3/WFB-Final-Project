Yo Soy La Chica Vegana

My name is Erica & I am a vegan foodie. I have been living the plant-based lifestyle for 2 years now. I come from a family of amazing cooks and was inspired from a young age, especially from watching & helping my mom create beautiful meals everyday for my family.

When I started my journey, I knew I wanted to continue to eat all of my favorite meals I used to enjoy, including the traditional Mexican ones I grew up eating. For that reason, I created this page to inspire others to cook simple, delious meals packed with plants.

Welcome to my page & I am so glad you are here!

-Erica
